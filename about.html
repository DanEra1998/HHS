Our Story

Herrera Handyman Service began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, quality help for everyday fixes and projects. Over the years, what started as a small service has grown into a trusted name for homeowners and businesses alike.

Why We Do It

We believe that every home and business deserves attention to detail and honest work. Our goal is to take the stress out of maintenance, one project at a time.

About the Owner

Daniel Herrera is a hands-on handyman with years of experience in painting, repairs, electrical work, and more. His passion for the craft and dedication to service are what make this business stand out.
