About Us

Herrera Handyman Service was founded with one simple goal: to bring trustworthy, professional repair and maintenance services to homeowners and small businesses. From humble beginnings to a growing, loyal client base, our mission remains the same — quality work, done right the first time.

Our Philosophy

We believe in treating your home like it's our own. That means clear communication, respect for your time and space, and always delivering what we promise — no shortcuts, no hidden costs.

Meet the Owners

Miguel Herrera brings over 25 years of hands-on experience in residential and commercial repair. From electrical work to drywall, Miguel’s commitment to excellence and genuine care for clients is what makes this service stand out.

His wife, Margarita Herrera, serves as co-owner and assistant. She manages business calls, schedules, and ensures every client receives prompt and professional communication throughout the project.
